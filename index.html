If you have already accepted a summer placement or 1-year placement but you haven't informed us, please log on to the Placement Database to enter/input your placement record. You can find the "My Placement Record" button on the menu pane at the Internship's Homepage.

Placement Record
To complete your placement record, please note that you MUST provide the following information:

  • Company Name
  • Job Title/Position
  • Nature of work (Detailed Job description)
  • Start Date
  • End Date
  • Working Location
  • Payment Type - either paid or unpaid or honorarium
  • Salary (if this is a paid internship or with honorarium)
  • Appointment letter (upload the appointment letter or offer letter or the contract as a proof of your internship)
  • Supervisor Name (you may send that later after you have started working), and
  • Supervisor Telephone/Email (you may send that later after you have started working).

The reasons to collect the information are:

  1. We need the information to justify whether this post satisfies the Internship requirements. Some of the posts may be advertised through us; however, we do not have the details of the job with us when we screened those positions and it is possible that the actual job duties are quite different from what have been advertised.
  2. For HKIE accreditation purpose - We are using this database for showing to HKIE during accreditation.

You may find that the status of your placement record is either NA / WAITING / INCOMPLETE / APPROVED. The initial status of every placement record is in NA, i.e., it is an empty record. Once you have inputted info to the placement record, the system will change the status to WAITING. After you have done (or at least after adding the Job Description), press the "Submit" button to inform me, and I shall work on the screening. When you have entered sufficient information, with the document(s) to prove the internship, and the job satisfies the training requirements, I shall change the status of your placement record to APPROVED. If there are missing information, I shall change the status of your placement record to INCOMPLETE, for example, missing job description, missing supervisor's information, and missing appointment document. In addition, I shall enter my remark on the "Remark" field to let you know what I am expecting you to follow up with.

Please note that to fulfill the Internship requirement, it is important to register with the department once you have secured a placement. If we do not have a record of your placement with us, you could not pass the Internship course.

Minimum Wages Ordinance
Please note that some companies may request you to prove that the internship program is a component of the university degree curriculum. The govt. has prepared a standard form - "Confirmation of 'student intern' status under the Minimum Wage Ordinance (Cap. 608)" - for this purpose.

If the company requests you to provide them as the proof in order to meet the hiring requirements, please following this procedure:
(1) Download a copy of the form from here; we have already filled in most information for you.
(2) Complete the form with necessary information.
(3) Submit the form to CS general office (to Ms. Smitty Lam)
(4) Smitty will call you to collect the form once I have signed it.
